What's Start-up Mentor ?

Start-up Mentor allows people who have an idea of start-up to get the help they need to create it.

Here is a platform which establishes relationships between future entrepreneurs and people who already created their structure.

The idea is simple: create some mutual support between entrepreneurs thanks to mentorship. Some of them can find new ideas and maybe collaborations or opportunities of investment, others can find help and expertise.
